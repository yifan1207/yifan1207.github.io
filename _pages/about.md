I am a student at University of California, Los Angeles studying Computer Science and Applied Mathematics. I am currently conducting independent research in **mechanistic interpretability**, developing methods to analyze how post-training reshapes model behavior via *first-divergence factorial diffing*.

My broader interests also include **reinforcement learning**, **multi-agent systems**, and **efficient ML systems**. I have worked on multi-agent component analysis, agent behavior monitoring & evaluation, and CPU-efficient systems (SimHash, int8 quantization, ONNX kernel fusion). 

Previously, I interned as an Applied Scientist at [Microsoft](https://www.microsoft.com/), working on SimHash and efficient ML systems. I have also worked at [Celestra](https://celestra.com/), [Judgment Labs](https://www.judgmentlabs.ai/), and [Tencent](https://www.tencent.com/en-us). I will be joining OpenAI (Integrity) as a Member of Technical Staff Intern in Summer 2026.
